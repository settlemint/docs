**Common Question**: "How can I start building blockchain applications so quickly? Most blockchain platforms require complex setup processes that take days or weeks."

### Account creation process (2 minutes)

<Steps>
<Step>

### Navigate to SettleMint Console

Visit [SettleMint Console](https://console.settlemint.com/) and enter your work email address.

**Why work email?** Enterprise features and collaboration tools require business email validation.

</Step>
<Step>

### Use passwordless authentication

Click **"Send Magic Link"** - no password required! A secure authentication link will be sent to your inbox for instant access.

**Alternative options**: Sign up using Google, GitHub, or Auth0 for even faster access.

</Step>
<Step>

### Claim your €250 free credits

First-time users automatically receive €250 in credits to explore all platform features without upfront costs.

**What can you build with €250?** A complete blockchain application with smart contracts, APIs, and frontend for 2-3 months.

</Step>
</Steps>

## How to set up your organization and billing (3 minutes)

<Callout type="question">
**Common Question**: "Do I need to pay upfront to start using SettleMint? What happens if I exceed my free credits?"

**Answer**: No upfront payment required! Your €250 free credits let you build and test extensively. Billing only starts when you exceed credits or deploy to production. Monthly billing on the 1st with transparent usage tracking.
