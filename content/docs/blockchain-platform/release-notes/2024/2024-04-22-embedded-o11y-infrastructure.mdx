## Embedded o11y infrastructure

![Changelog Image](/images/release-notes/embedded-o11y-infrastructure.png)

The platform now includes a complete observability suite. It powers the resource
metrics and logs in the platform UI, and includes a Grafana instance for
operation personel to track any metric they wish to follow.
