## Base architecture and operations

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Sequencing Layer**

    Coinbase manages the current sequencer, batching transactions for optimistic settlement while sharing roadmap updates toward permissionless sequencing.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    **Rollup Protocol**

    The OP Stack execution layer retains full EVM equivalence, ensuring that Solidity contracts behave identically to Ethereum mainnet deployments.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    **Fraud Proofs**

    Challenges leverage Optimism's upgradeable fault-proof system, providing recourse during disputes and aligning with future multi-proof enhancements.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    **Operational Tooling**

    Coinbase Developer Platform delivers observability, incident response, and integration guides, streamlining Day 2 operations for enterprise teams.
  </Step>
</Steps>
