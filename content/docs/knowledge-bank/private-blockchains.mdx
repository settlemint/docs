# Private Blockchain Networks

<Callout type="info">
  Private blockchains are permissioned networks where participation is
  controlled and restricted to authorized participants.
</Callout>

## Major Private Networks

## Key Features

### 1. Access Control

- Permissioned participation
- Known identities
- Role-based access

### 2. Performance

- Higher throughput
- Lower latency
- Configurable consensus

### 3. Privacy

- Restricted data access
- Private transactions
- Confidential contracts

## Network Types

### Consortium Networks

- Multiple organizations
- Shared governance
- Agreed-upon rules
- Cross-organization collaboration

### Single Organization

- Internal deployment
- Full control
- Custom configuration
- Department-level access

## Use Cases

1. **Financial Services**

   - Settlement systems
   - Trade finance
   - Asset tracking

2. **Supply Chain**

   - Product traceability
   - Supplier management
   - Quality assurance

3. **Healthcare**
   - Patient records
   - Drug supply chain
   - Clinical trials
